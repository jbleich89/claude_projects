Year-by-year simulation of taxes, balances, RMDs, and heir after-tax wealth under the SECURE 10-yr rule. Pits every strategy head-to-head, tuned for MFJ → survivor transitions. Drop a config.js next to this file to pre-fill your own numbers.

Returns & inflation (asset location)
Defaults: IRA and Roth both 7.5% (equity-like, no asset-location arbitrage assumed), Taxable 6% (tax-efficient balanced, drag from dividends/interest). Edit to test asset-location effect.
Heirs
Inherited IRA distributed straight-line over 10 years per SECURE Act. Inherited Roth distributed in year 10 (tax-free). Taxable account receives step-up at 2nd death.
Conversion strategy
IRMAA MFJ 2026 tiers (Part B + Part D surcharge per person): ≤$218K (std, $0) · ≤$274K (+$95.70/mo) · ≤$342K (+$240.40/mo) · ≤$410K (+$385.00/mo) · ≤$750K (+$533.10/mo). Default $342K fully uses tier 2 (+$240.40/mo × 2 ≈ $5,770/yr once both on Medicare) while staying out of tier 3.
Conversions stop once both pre-tax IRAs are drained.
